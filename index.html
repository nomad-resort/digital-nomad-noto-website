Access

Just hours from the city — yet a world away.

From Haneda(Tokyo)

  1. Haneda → Noto Satoyama Airport: about 1 hour (by plane)
  2. Airport → Nanao city: 40–50 minutes (by car)

From Tokyo

  1. Tokyo → Kanazawa: about 2h 30min (by Hokuriku Shinkansen)
  2. Kanazawa → Nanao: about 60–80min (by JR Nanao Line)

From Osaka

  1. Osaka → Tsuruga: about 1h 20–30min (by Limited Express Thunderbird)
  2. Tsuruga → Kanazawa: about 1h (by Hokuriku Shinkansen)
  3. Kanazawa → Nanao: 60–80min (by JR Nanao Line)

Car Driving

Nanao → Notojima, Anamizu, Suzu, etc. : 20–40 min by car.

The drive itself feels like part of the scenery.
